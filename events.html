caprera openluchttheater & wandelpark

Caprera walking park, a 40-hectare natural sanctuary situated on the slopes of the Kopje van Bloemendaal, South Kennemerland's highest dune peak, is a vital component of the Zuid-Kennemerland National Park, and it's conveniently located just 8 minutes from Overveen by bike click here to see the location . Open year-round between sunrise and sunset, it offers serene walks for visitors with or without dogs, with an entrance cost of just one euro, a nominal fee considering the abundant enjoyment it offers. Caprera also hosts open-air events, shows, and parties several times a year; you can check here the schedule on their website for more details.

Celebrate National Holidays and Festivals
